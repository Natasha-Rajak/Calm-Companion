Escape the noise, Embrace the calm

Calm Companion is your personal mental wellness space designed to help you unwind, reflect, and find peace in a fast-moving world.

Key Features

Music Lounge

Access curated playlists for relaxation, focus, and sleep through embedded Spotify integration.

Meditation

Guided meditations and breathing exercises via embedded YouTube videos.

Inspirational Quotes

Daily positive affirmations and quotes to uplift your mood and mindset.

Gratitude Wall

A personal space to record and reflect on things you're grateful for.

AI Chatbot

A supportive AI companion to talk to without judgment whenever you need.

User Profile

Personalized experience with your own account and preferences.

Reduced Stress & Anxiety

Improved Focus

Increased Positivity

Uplift your mood with daily affirmations and gratitude practice.

Emotional Balance
